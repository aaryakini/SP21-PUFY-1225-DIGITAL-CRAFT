Project Instructions

Work with your homework partner and search the internet for websites you find interesting. Pick two and break them into structural blocks like we discussed in class. Submit the url, a screen grab and a brief written description of the website and why you chose it. Also include how it should be broken up structurally, include drawings or visual references as needed.
